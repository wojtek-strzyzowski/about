CH Bank

About Us

CH is the leading bank with a global network and firmly rooted in Europe. The bank is concentrating on its strengths - in its corporate bank, which was newly formed in 2019, in a leading private customer bank, in a focused investment bank and in asset management.

History

CH has been represented in Switzerland for more than 40 years and has strong roots as one of the most important foreign banks with around 600 employees in Zurich and Geneva. As a globally networked and represented universal bank, it offers all services locally across the four areas of private customer bank, corporate bank, investment bank and DWS, the bank's asset management division. Switzerland is the center for the asset management business in the Central Europe region. In addition, parts of the Asia-Pacific (APAC), Middle East & Africa (MEA) and Latin America (LatAm) business are managed from Switzerland.
